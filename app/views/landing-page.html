Our Constitution

Our Constitution, developed with the engagement of system partners and other stakeholders, sets out our purpose, powers, and governance and leadership arrangements to ensure the effective discharge of our duties and responsibilities.

The Health and Care Act (2022) requires each Integrated Care Board (ICB) to have a Constitution.

Our Constitution, developed with our North East London Integrated Care System partners and other stakeholders, sets out our purpose, powers, and governance and leadership arrangements to ensure the effective discharge of our duties and responsibilities. 

Our Constitution is therefore an important document that sets out what we do and how we work.

  • NHS North East London Integrated Care Board Constitution

  • Standing orders

    Part of our Constitution, these standing orders are the written rules which regulate the proceedings of NHS North East London.

NHS Constitution

The NHS Constitution aims to safeguard the principles and values of the NHS in England. 

Read the NHS Constitution. 

You can also read the Handbook to the NHS constitution, which explains the rights to which patients, public and staff are entitled, and the pledges set out in the Constitution. It also outlines the roles we all play in supporting the NHS.
